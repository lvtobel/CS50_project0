The Opening

There are only few moves that are typically played as the first move with white. Those are the following:
  • e4
  • d4
  • c4
  • Nf3

In fact, if we want to consider how these moves compare to each other and other opening moves, we can look at the games played on Lichess, a very popular chess server. These are the stats as of April 16th, 2020:

Opening move Games Played Percentage
e4 1038424 45.6%
d4 826559 36.3%
Nf3 235289 10.3%
c4 158750 7.0%
g3 18614 0.8%

The drastic drop from c4 (7.0%) to g3 (0.8%) shows how popular those four first moves are.
